Zoho Inventory is a cloud-based inventory and order management system designed to help businesses track stock, manage warehouses, and streamline fulfillment. Shopify, on the other hand, is one of the world’s leading eCommerce platforms used by millions of merchants to sell products online.

When integrated through SKUPlugs, these two systems work together as a unified solution. The integration automatically syncs essential business data, ensuring that both systems remain updated without manual intervention.
